Conclusion

It is hard to say that life expectancy is related to the GDP only with this data. However, we can see the tendency that two factors have the same direction. Espeically, African countries had civil wars are showing the tendency clearly. Although, I didn't go far till doing statistical analysis, there seems quite clear relationships between life expectancy and GDP among those countries. Meanwhile, the case of developed countries, its life expectancy grows gradually without showing relationship with GDP. OECD countries already had near 70 life expectancy at 1960.
